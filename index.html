About

Unlocking Growth Potential

Ashika Private Equity Advisors through its first fund Ashika Growth Equity Fund I aims to unlock growth potential in manufacturing and consumer sectors.

With our focus on innovation, operational excellence and sustainable business practices, we will partner with pioneering organizations to accelerate growth through strategic value creation and our deep sectoral understanding.

Approach

We invest in:

People

High-quality founders and leadership teams. Strong, visionary leaders who drive innovation and sustainable growth

Product-Market Fit

Businesses with proven Product-Market-Fit (PMF) and established customer validation

Competitive Edge

Businesses with differentiated market positioning with either a process or product moat

Governance

Businesses with clean, transparent corporate practices and robust controls

We don't invest in:

Businesses with inherently unprofitable unit economics with focus only on revenue growth and no clear path to profitability

Businesses with questionable founder track record

Businesses with high risk of capital erosion

Investment Criteria

B2B / B2C manufacturing and consumer companies focused on innovation and growth

Primarily headquartered in India

Revenue range from INR 50 cr to INR 500 cr

Profitable at unit economics level with clear path to profitability or profitable at the P&L level
